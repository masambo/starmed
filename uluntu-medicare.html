Corporate Social Responsibility

Healthcare as a Civic Duty

Uluntu Medicare was born from the belief that access to healthcare is not a privilege; it is a right. Through this initiative, Star Medical Services actively works to close the gap between those with medical aid and those without, directly reducing the burden on public health infrastructure.

Community Impact

Thousands of Namibian families who previously had no access to structured healthcare can now receive consistent, quality medical consultations at a predictable monthly cost. Uluntu brings stability to community health.

Inclusive Access

From domestic workers to small business owners, Uluntu Medicare is designed to serve everyone. No complicated procedures and no exclusions based on pre-existing conditions for primary care. Just straightforward, inclusive healthcare.

Sustainable Health

By enabling preventive care and routine follow-ups, Uluntu reduces emergency health crises before they occur. This preventive-first approach relieves pressure on state hospitals and builds a healthier, more productive Namibia.
